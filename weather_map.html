Old West Christmas Light Fest

The Old West Christmas Light Fest will have a drive-thru light displays and an Old West town filled with lights, food and fun at an all-new location. Guests can enjoy dazzling lights, visit Santa, snap holiday photos and travel back in time with games, activities and family fun, including Santa’s Holly Jolly Herd of Longhorns, ready to pull his sleigh across Texas.
